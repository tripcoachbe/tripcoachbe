FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Magic Truffles are safe and non-addictive. There is no known case of overdose. Nevertheless, these are very powerful plant medicines! Therefore it is highty recommended to use these is a safe environment under supervision of a experienced 'tripsitter'.


In Belgium it is illegal to posses, sell or produce any psychedelic substances. In the Netherlands the Magic Truffle is an exception to these regulations. That is why the trip sessions of TripCoach.be take place just across the Dutch border.


Psychedelic truffles are the underground fungal mass of certain species of psychedelic mushrooms which contain the same psychoactive ingredient called psilocybin.

Apart from the psychedelic effects, some people can feel nausea right after ingesting Magic Truffles. Making thee from the truffles greatly reduces the chance of nausea.


There are some contra-indications for the use of Magic Truffles. Detailed information about this topic can be found in our e-book.


Research has shown that about 90% of test subjects experience a psychedelic trip after ingestion of psilocybin. For about 10% there is a reduced response. In many cases this can be compensated by increasing the dose during the session.


You have to wait for 12 hours after ingesting Magic Truffles before you can safely drive a vehicle. For this reason TripCoach.be offers the possibility to spend the night in the Netherlands. The next morning you can safely drive.


After the trip ends, you are back to normal. All physical effects psilocybin are gone after 12 hours. Nevertheless, the intensity of the experience and the emotions will have a lasting impression on you for the weeks or months to come. Research has indicated that participants of a psychedelic trip temporary show more openess and less rigidity in their personality.
